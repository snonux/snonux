Cinzel and Spectral
===================

This directory bundles the web fonts:

    cinzel-v26-latin_latin-ext-regular.woff2     (weight 400)
    spectral-v15-latin_latin-ext-regular.woff2   (weight 400)
    spectral-v15-latin_latin-ext-600.woff2       (weight 600)

Subsets: latin, latin-ext.
