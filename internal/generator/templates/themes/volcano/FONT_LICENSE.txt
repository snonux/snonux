Volcano theme fonts
===================

This directory bundles the web fonts:

    bebas-neue-v16-latin_latin-ext-regular.woff2  (weight 400)
    inter-v20-latin_latin-ext-regular.woff2       (weight 400)
    inter-v20-latin_latin-ext-600.woff2           (weight 600)

Subsets: latin, latin-ext.
