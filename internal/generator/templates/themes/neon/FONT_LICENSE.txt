Neon theme font
===============

This directory bundles the web fonts:

    orbitron-v35-latin_latin-ext-regular.woff2  (weight 400)
    orbitron-v35-latin_latin-ext-500.woff2      (weight 500)
    orbitron-v35-latin_latin-ext-700.woff2      (weight 700)

Subsets:

    Orbitron latin, latin-ext
